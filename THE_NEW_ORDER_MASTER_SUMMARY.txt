ORGANIZATIONS SYSTEM
==================================================

Unique Concept:

Organizations are created to solve specific problems.

If Organization A accuses Organization B:

1. Independent Investigation Organization created
2. Evidence collected
3. Findings published
4. Solution implemented
5. Investigation organization dissolved
   OR becomes permanent if required
