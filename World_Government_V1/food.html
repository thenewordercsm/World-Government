FOOD DEPARTMENT

Food Security For Every Citizen

Mission

The Food Department ensures that every citizen has access to sufficient, affordable and nutritious food. Its responsibilities include production, storage, transportation and distribution.

FOOD INFRASTRUCTURE

🌾 Government Farms

Large scale farms operated to guarantee national food security.

🏢 Hydroponic Towers

Urban vertical farms producing crops year round using modern technology.

🔬 Agricultural Research

Research into better crops, higher yields and sustainability.

🚚 Distribution Network

Transportation systems delivering food across the country.

🏪 Emergency Reserves

Food reserves for droughts, disasters and emergencies.

💧 Water Coordination

Works closely with the Water Department for irrigation and supply.
