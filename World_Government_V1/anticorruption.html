ANTI-CORRUPTION DEPARTMENT

Transparency • Accountability • Oversight

Mission

The Anti-Corruption Department exists to ensure that all organizations operate honestly, efficiently and in the public interest. No department is above investigation. Every organization is subject to review.

CORE RESPONSIBILITIES

🔍 Investigations

Investigate corruption, abuse of authority, resource misuse and misconduct.

📊 Audits

Conduct regular financial, operational and performance audits.

📢 Transparency

Publish reports and findings for public review.

📨 Citizen Complaints

Receive and evaluate reports from citizens.

⚖ Corrective Actions

Recommend reforms and structural changes.

🛡 Oversight

Monitor all departments continuously.

WHO WATCHES THE WATCHERS?
