WATER DEPARTMENT

Water Security For Every Citizen

Mission

The Water Department manages water collection, storage, treatment and distribution. Its goal is to guarantee reliable water access for agriculture, industry and households.

WATER INFRASTRUCTURE

🏞 Multi-Dam Construction

Strategic dam networks for storage, power generation and flood control.

💧 Reservoir Systems

Long-term water storage for drought protection.

🚰 Water Treatment

Purification and safe drinking water systems.

🌾 Irrigation Networks

Water delivery systems supporting farms.

🌊 Flood Management

Monitoring and prevention of flood disasters.

🏭 Desalination

Converting seawater into usable freshwater.
