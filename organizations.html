HOW ORGANIZATIONS KEEP EACH OTHER IN CHECK

Oversight Reviews

Organizations regularly review the performance of related organizations.

Transparency Reports

Major decisions and performance metrics are publicly available.

Independent Audits

Independent audit organizations inspect operations and efficiency.

Citizen Feedback

Citizens can report problems and suggest improvements.

WHEN MULTIPLE ORGANIZATIONS FAIL

Systemic Failure

A problem affects multiple organizations simultaneously.

Special Investigation

A dedicated task force investigates the entire system.

Structural Reform

Organizations may be redesigned, merged or replaced.

Long-Term Monitoring

Results are monitored to prevent future failures.

EXAMPLE CASE

Food Department

Reports food shortages.

Water Department

Claims water supply is sufficient.

Independent Investigation

Reviews farms, reservoirs, distribution and usage.

Solution

Infrastructure improvements and new monitoring systems.
